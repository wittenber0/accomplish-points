## Overview

Mary provides professional consulting services bringing strategic, technical, and experiential expertise to support and navigate organizations and communities to do positive work. Services are customized, scoped for and with each client to support the people and objectives of the organization and facilitate their own excellence.

Services can be scoped for a range of circumstances including complex ongoing processes with multiple interests and controversial decision-making, or can be more focused on singular issues or meetings.

**Areas of specialty include:** urban and rural planning; land use and real estate analysis; natural resources, parks and recreations; agriculture; transportation and libraries; engagement and decision making; community and organizational development; scenario backcasting and forecasting; budget implications; governance and management; program and project design and implementation.

## Engagement Models

> Source: Slide 47

Mary works in two service models:

### 1. External Consultant
Leaders hire Mary to provide expertise in listening, assessing and analyzing needs and partnering to accomplish what is most important. This role allows Mary agility and support for people's growth and teamwork and often a client candor that facilitates evolution and change. The constraints of "in-house" role are removed and specific change and growth targets are more available. Re-imagining and re-constructing, the smart risk taking of "outside the box" viewing and drafting scenarios are of tremendous value.

### 2. Internal Staff (Interim / Temp / Part-Time)
Mary serves from an appointment within an organization with a dutiful and enthusiastic alignment with staff and leaders to deliver excellence within an organization. The roles and responsibilities within the organizational framework empower a staff position. Mary serves internally when that internal operations role is valuable to accomplish what is needed.
